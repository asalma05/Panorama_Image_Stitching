Dataset Description
===================

Three sets of real overlapping photographs captured for panoramic stitching.
Processing order in the notebook: data3 → data1 → data2 (ascending image count).

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
data3/  (2 images)  –  Coastal Sunset Panorama
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Files:
  IMG_6625.jpg  –  Left panel: rocky coastline with warm orange sunset glow,
                   foreground rocks and breaking surf visible.
  IMG_6626.jpg  –  Right panel: continuation looking out to open sea,
                   cooler tones, distant headland on horizon.

Scene:  Seaside location photographed at dusk, camera panned horizontally
        from left (coast) to right (open water).
Overlap: Approximately 25–35% horizontal overlap between the two frames.
Challenge: Large uniform sky and sea areas are low in texture; features
           must be found on rocks, surf patterns, and the horizon edge.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
data1/  (3 images)  –  Elevated Coastal Panorama
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Files (ordered left → right):
  IMG_6622.PNG  –  Left panel: coast and fields visible, warm sunset sky.
  IMG_6623.PNG  –  Centre panel: overlaps both neighbours ~30%.
  IMG_6624.PNG  –  Right panel: continuation rightward along the coast.

Scene:  Elevated vantage point overlooking a coastal landscape at golden hour,
        camera panned from left to right.
Format: Portrait-orientation iPhone photos with black letterbox bars at top
        and bottom (added by the iOS screenshot mechanism).
Preprocessing: The notebook automatically detects and crops these black borders
               before feature detection.
Overlap: ~30% between consecutive frames.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
data2/  (4 images)  –  Lake Annecy (Esplanade des Marquisats)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Files (ordered left → right):
  IMG_6627.PNG  –  Panel 1: lake surface with white buoys, mountains behind;
                   NOTE: this image is an iOS Photos app screenshot — it
                   contains phone UI chrome (status bar, navigation bar, bottom
                   controls). The notebook applies a dedicated crop to remove
                   the UI before processing.
  IMG_6628.PNG  –  Panel 2: continuation along the lake, overlaps panel 1.
  IMG_6629.PNG  –  Panel 3: further right along the promenade.
  IMG_6630.PNG  –  Panel 4: rightmost panel showing the dock/mooring area.

Scene:  Lac d'Annecy photographed from the Esplanade des Marquisats on
        1 February 2025 at 5:48 PM. Mountains and lake visible throughout.
Overlap: ~30–40% between consecutive frames.
Challenge: Large expanse of uniform lake surface; mountains and buoys are
           the primary feature-rich regions.
